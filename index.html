Frequently Asked Questions

What condition are the clothes in?

All our clothing items are carefully inspected and cleaned before listing. We only accept gently used items in excellent condition with minimal signs of wear. Each item is photographed accurately to show its true condition.

How do I know if an item will fit me?

Each item includes detailed measurements and sizing information. We recommend checking the measurements against your own clothes for the best fit. Our customer service team is also happy to help with sizing questions.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for items that don't fit or aren't as described. Items must be in the same condition as received. Return shipping costs are covered by us if the item was misrepresented.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 2-5 business days within Botswana. Orders over P200 qualify for free delivery. We also offer express delivery for urgent orders at an additional cost.

Can I sell my clothes to OnlyThrifts?

Yes! We have a clothing exchange program where you can trade in your gently used items for store credit. Contact us to learn more about our acceptance criteria and the process.

Are your prices negotiable?

Our prices are already set at competitive thrift rates. However, we occasionally offer bundle deals and seasonal discounts. Follow our social media for special promotions and sales events.
